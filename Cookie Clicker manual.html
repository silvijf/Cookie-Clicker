Cookie Clicker manual

A cursor increases your cps by 1.

A granny increases your cps by 10.

A farm increases your cps by 80.

A mine increases your cps by 470.

A factory increases your cps by 2600.

A bank increases your cps by 14,000.

A temple increases your cps by 78,000.

A wizard tower increases your cps by 440,000.


The game auto-saves every 3 seconds if auto-save is enabled. Recommed is to save manually by hitting the save button before leaving. You have to load your progress manually by hitting the load button.


Cps stands for cookies per second.


IMPORTANT!!! ALWAYS FIRST LOAD AND THEN ENABLE AUTOSAVE, OTHERWISE, A BLANK PROGRESS WILL BE AUTOSAVED!
